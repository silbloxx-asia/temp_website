Silbloxx Asia is the Vietnamese arm of Silbloxx.

We are part of an international industrial group, BRIAM, active in food and feed infrastructure projects worldwide. From Vietnam, we support global silo projects through manufacturing-related activities and project support, in line with the group's technical frameworks and quality standards.

Our team in Vietnam collaborates closely with colleagues across the international BRIAM network, combining global experience with strong local execution.

Our Silo Solutions

Silbloxx develops and delivers modular square silo systems for the food and feed industry worldwide.

These systems are the result of in-house product development, long-term engineering expertise, and continuous optimisation for industrial production.

We operate as part of an integrated international value chain, working closely with engineering, procurement, and production partners across the Silbloxx and BRIAM network.
